Trends and Challenges of Arabic Chatbots Literature Review

Authors: Yassine Saoudi, Mohamed Mohsen Gammoudi

Journal: Jordanian Journal of Computers and Information Technology

Volume/Issue: 09(03)

Publication Date: September 2023

Download PDF

Abstract: This paper presents a comprehensive qualitative analysis of the strengths and limitations of Arabic conversational AI systems, and discusses history, classification, language challenges, rule-based, retrieval-based and deep learning-based approaches, as well as future research directions.
